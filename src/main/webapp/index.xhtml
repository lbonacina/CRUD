Simple example to demostrate CRUD operations on simple object, either as a single page or as a two page "list-edit" The beans to control the one and two pages example are very similar: - on the one page example the bean is view scoped (CODI ViewAccesScoped) - on the two page example the bean is a conversation scoped bean (CODI ConversationScoped) (conversation should be closed explicitly) Warning: a CDI Interception will break the "destroy" part of the bean with a bug (NullPointerException)
